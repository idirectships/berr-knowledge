# Why is WhatsApp's privacy policy facing a legal challenge in India?

WhatsApp's 2021 privacy policy update, which requires users to share data with Meta for advertising, is facing legal challenges in India over concerns about user consent and data protection. The case highlights growing regulatory scrutiny of tech companies' data practices.
