# Brent crude hits $100 a barrel as reserve release plans fail to ease Iran war-led supply worries

Brent crude oil prices surged to $100 a barrel as concerns over potential supply disruptions from the Iran conflict outweighed efforts by the IEA to ease markets through a 400 million barrel reserve release. The move highlights growing fears over global energy security.
