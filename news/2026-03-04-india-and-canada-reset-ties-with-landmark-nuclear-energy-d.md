# India and Canada reset ties with 'landmark' nuclear energy deal

India and Canada have announced a landmark nuclear energy deal, including uranium supply agreements, during a high-level meeting between Prime Minister Narendra Modi and Canadian Prime Minister Mark Carney in Delhi. The deal signals a significant reset in bilateral relations and enhances India's energy security.
