# Iran War Is Causing Biggest-Ever Oil Market Disruption, IEA Says

The International Energy Agency reports that the Iran war is causing the largest oil market disruption in history, affecting 7.5% of global supply and significantly impacting exports. The conflict has immediate implications for global energy prices and market stability.
