# New US ambassador to South Africa summoned over 'undiplomatic remarks'

The new U.S. ambassador to South Africa, Leo Brent Bozell, was summoned by South African officials over remarks deemed undiplomatic, relating to the country's apartheid history. The incident highlights tensions in diplomatic relations and the sensitivity of historical issues in international diplomacy.
