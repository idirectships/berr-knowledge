# US missile hit military base near Iran school, video analysis shows

A US Tomahawk missile struck a military base near a primary school in southern Iran, according to video analysis, where Iranian authorities reported 168 deaths. The incident raises concerns over civilian casualties and escalates tensions between the US and Iran.
