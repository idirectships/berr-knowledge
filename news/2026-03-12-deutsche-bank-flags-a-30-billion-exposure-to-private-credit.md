# Deutsche Bank Flags a $30 Billion Exposure to Private Credit

Deutsche Bank disclosed a €26 billion exposure to private credit, highlighting risks from redemptions, regulatory scrutiny, and AI's impact on borrowers. The revelation underscores growing concerns over the stability of the private credit sector.
