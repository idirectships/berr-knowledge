# Indian Assets Crack as Energy Shock Brings Growth Fears to Fore

Indian assets are declining amid an energy shock from the Iran war, fueling growth concerns and pushing the rupee to record lows as foreign investors remain cautious. The situation highlights vulnerability in India's energy-dependent economy.
