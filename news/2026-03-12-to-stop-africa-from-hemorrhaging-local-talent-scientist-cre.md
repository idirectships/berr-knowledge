# To stop Africa from hemorrhaging local talent, scientist creates drug discovery lab

A Zambian scientist has launched a drug discovery lab in South Africa to retain local talent and reduce brain drain on the continent. The initiative aims to foster scientific innovation and economic development within Africa.
