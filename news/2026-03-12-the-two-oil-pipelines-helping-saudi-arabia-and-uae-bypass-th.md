# The two oil pipelines helping Saudi Arabia and UAE bypass the Strait of Hormuz

Saudi Arabia and the UAE are utilizing alternative oil pipelines to bypass the Strait of Hormuz, reducing reliance on a critical but vulnerable shipping route. This move could impact global oil flows and regional energy security amid tensions with Iran.
