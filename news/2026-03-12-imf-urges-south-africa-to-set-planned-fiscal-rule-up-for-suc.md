# IMF Urges South Africa to Set Planned Fiscal Rule Up for Success

The IMF has advised South Africa that its planned fiscal rule could enhance policy credibility but requires substantial support to be effective. The recommendation highlights the importance of structural reforms in managing the country's public finances.
