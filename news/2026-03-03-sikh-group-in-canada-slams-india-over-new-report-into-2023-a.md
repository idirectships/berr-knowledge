# Sikh group in Canada slams India over new report into 2023 activist killing

A Sikh group in Canada has condemned India following a report alleging Indian consular officials in Vancouver were involved in the 2023 assassination of activist Hardeep Singh Nijjar. The claim could strain diplomatic relations between Canada and India.
