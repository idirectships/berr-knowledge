# India’s consumer inflation rises to 3.21% in February as oil risks loom

India's consumer inflation rose to 3.21% in February, driven by global energy price pressures, though the central bank is unlikely to act amid ongoing risks from the Hormuz Strait. The data highlights the impact of global oil markets on domestic economic policy.
