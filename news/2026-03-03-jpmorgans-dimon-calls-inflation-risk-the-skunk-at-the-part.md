# JPMorgan's Dimon Calls Inflation Risk the ‘Skunk at the Party’

JPMorgan Chase CEO Jamie Dimon warns that inflation could pose a significant risk to the US economy, comparing it to a "skunk at the party" during a conference in Miami. His remarks highlight ongoing concerns about economic stability amid current conditions.
