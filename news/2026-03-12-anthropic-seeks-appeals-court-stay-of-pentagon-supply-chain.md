# Anthropic seeks appeals court stay of Pentagon supply-chain risk designation

Anthropic is seeking a stay from an appeals court to block the Pentagon's designation of its AI tools as a supply-chain risk, following a dispute over technology guardrails for military use. The legal battle could impact AI regulation and defense sector collaboration.
