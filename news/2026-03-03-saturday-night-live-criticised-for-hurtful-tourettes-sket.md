# Saturday Night Live criticised for 'hurtful' Tourette's sketch

"Saturday Night Live" faces criticism from the Tourette Action charity for a sketch deemed "hurtful" and inaccurate in its portrayal of Tourette's syndrome, continuing the show's recent controversies. The incident highlights ongoing concerns about the representation of neurological conditions in media.
