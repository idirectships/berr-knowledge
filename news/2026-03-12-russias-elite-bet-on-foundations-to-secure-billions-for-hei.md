# Russia’s Elite Bet on Foundations to Secure Billions For Heirs

Wealthy Russians are establishing private foundations to safeguard and transfer their assets domestically, leveraging new policies aimed at repatriating overseas wealth. The move highlights efforts by Russia’s elite to secure their fortunes amid ongoing economic and geopolitical pressures.
