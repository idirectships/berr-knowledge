# Record Oil Shock From Iran War Tests Crisis Tools

A potential war with Iran could trigger a significant oil supply disruption, raising concerns about the effectiveness of emergency stockpile releases in stabilizing markets. The situation tests existing crisis management tools and highlights vulnerabilities in global energy security.
