# India Turns to Dirty Fuels to Fight Crippling Supply Squeeze

India is increasing reliance on polluting fuels like biomass and fuel oil due to gas shortages exacerbated by the war, impacting both households and industries. The shift highlights energy security challenges and environmental concerns.
