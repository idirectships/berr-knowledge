# UBS Likes Gold, Dollar, Inflation-Protected Bonds Amid Heightened Risk

UBS Wealth Management's Mark Andersen recommends gold, the U.S. dollar, and inflation-protected bonds as hedges against geopolitical risks amid the Middle East conflict. The advice reflects a shift in portfolio strategies due to heightened global uncertainties.
