# Japan Insurers Still Keen on Private Debt Amid Growing Red Flags

Japan's largest life insurers are increasing private credit investments despite growing concerns about the sector's stability, signaling continued confidence in the asset class. The move highlights potential risks and opportunities in Japan's financial markets.
