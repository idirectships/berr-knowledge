# Trump admin estimates US war on Iran cost $11.3bn in first 6 days: Report

The Trump administration estimates the U.S. war on Iran has already cost $11.3 billion in the first six days, with officials projecting an additional $50 billion may be required. Lawmakers have raised concerns over the financial implications.
