# BMW Projects Flat Automaking Margin Over Tariff Costs

BMW AG anticipates flat automaking margins in 2026 due to rising tariff costs and increased competition in China, signaling ongoing challenges for the luxury automaker. The outlook highlights the impact of global trade policies on automotive profitability.
