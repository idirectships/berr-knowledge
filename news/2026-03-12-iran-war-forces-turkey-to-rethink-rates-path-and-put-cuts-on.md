# Iran War Forces Turkey to Rethink Rates Path and Put Cuts on Ice

Rising energy prices due to an Iran war have forced Turkey to reconsider its interest-rate cutting cycle, potentially delaying planned monetary easing. The move highlights the economic risks posed by regional conflicts.
