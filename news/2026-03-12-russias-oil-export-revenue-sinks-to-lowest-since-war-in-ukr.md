# Russia’s Oil-Export Revenue Sinks to Lowest Since War in Ukraine

Russia’s oil-export revenue fell to its lowest level since the start of the war in Ukraine, driven by Western sanctions and attacks on oil infrastructure, according to the International Energy Agency. The decline highlights the economic impact of the conflict and ongoing Western pressure.
