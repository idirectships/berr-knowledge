# EU Countries Call for Stronger European Payments Systems

EU countries are pushing for stronger domestic payments systems and faster deployment of the digital euro to reduce reliance on foreign platforms and enhance economic growth. The move signals a strategic shift in Europe’s financial infrastructure.
