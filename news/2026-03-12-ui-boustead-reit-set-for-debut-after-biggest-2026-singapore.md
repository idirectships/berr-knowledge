# UI Boustead REIT Set for Debut After Biggest 2026 Singapore IPO

UI Boustead REIT is set to debut on the Singapore stock exchange following a $764 million IPO, the largest in the city-state this year. The offering reflects strong investor demand for real estate investment trusts in the region.
