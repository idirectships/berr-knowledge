# Trump raises the stakes on China with Section 301 trade probe, weeks before Beijing summit

Former President Donald Trump has initiated a Section 301 trade probe against China ahead of an upcoming Beijing summit, escalating tensions ahead of potential high-level diplomatic discussions. The move could impact U.S.-China trade relations and influence negotiations.
