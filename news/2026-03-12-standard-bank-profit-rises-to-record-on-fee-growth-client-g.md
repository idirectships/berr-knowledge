# Standard Bank Profit Rises to Record on Fee Growth, Client Gains

Standard Bank Group Ltd. reported a record profit driven by fee growth and an expanding client base, with market volatility also boosting trading revenue. The results highlight strong performance in Africa’s largest lender by assets.
