# India in Talks With Iran to Secure Safe Passage for Tankers

India is in discussions with Iran to ensure the safe passage of over 20 tankers through the Strait of Hormuz, signaling potential diplomatic efforts to stabilize regional shipping routes amid ongoing tensions. The move could impact global oil trade and regional security dynamics.
