# EU Weighs Looser Carbon Rules and State Aid to Cut Energy Prices

The European Commission is exploring relaxed carbon-permit rules and increased state aid to address rising energy prices, signaling a potential shift in EU climate policy amid economic pressure. The move could impact emissions targets and energy markets across Europe.
