# Duke extends No. 1 record in AP Top 25 as Michigan State, Nebraska and Texas Tech crash top 10

Duke remains No. 1 in the AP Top 25 for the second consecutive week, extending its record for most top-10 appearances. Michigan State, Nebraska, and Texas Tech fell out of the top 10.
