# Mali to Introduce Special Mining Brigade to Curb Illegal Output

Mali is creating a special mining brigade with judicial powers to monitor gold mining and quarrying sites, aiming to reduce illegal output and boost government revenue. The move signals a crackdown on illicit mining activities that have undermined state control and fueled corruption.
