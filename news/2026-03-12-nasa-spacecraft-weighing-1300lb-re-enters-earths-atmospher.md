# Nasa spacecraft weighing 1,300lb re-enters Earth's atmosphere

A NASA spacecraft weighing 1,300 pounds is re-entering Earth's atmosphere, with a low risk of debris reaching the surface. The event highlights the routine nature of satellite re-entry and the measures taken to mitigate risks.
