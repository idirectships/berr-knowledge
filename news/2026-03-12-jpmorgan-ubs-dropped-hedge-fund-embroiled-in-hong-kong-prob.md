# JPMorgan, UBS Dropped Hedge Fund Embroiled in Hong Kong Probe

JPMorgan and UBS terminated their prime brokerage relationships with a Hong Kong hedge fund under investigation for alleged insider trading, prior to the probe being made public. The move highlights potential regulatory and reputational risks for financial institutions involved with the fund.
