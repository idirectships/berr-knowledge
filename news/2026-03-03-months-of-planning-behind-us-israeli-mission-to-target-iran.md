# Months of planning behind US-Israeli mission to target Iran's supreme leader

U.S. and Israeli intelligence agencies conducted months of planning for a mission targeting Iran's supreme leader, with critical intelligence obtained hours before an attack on a compound in central Tehran. The operation highlights escalating tensions between the U.S., Israel, and Iran.
