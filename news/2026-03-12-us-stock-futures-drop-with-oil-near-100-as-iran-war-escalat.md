# US Stock Futures Drop With Oil Near $100 as Iran War Escalates

US stock futures fell as tensions between Iran and the US escalated, sending oil prices near $100 a barrel amid fears of disrupted supply. The escalation raises concerns over global energy markets and economic stability.
