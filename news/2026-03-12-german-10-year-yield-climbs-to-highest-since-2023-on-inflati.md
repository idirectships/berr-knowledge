# German 10-Year Yield Climbs to Highest Since 2023 on Inflation Fears

German 10-year bond yields rose to their highest level since October 2023 amid inflation concerns driven by rising energy prices and the escalating war in Iran. The move signals growing investor anxiety over economic stability in the eurozone.
