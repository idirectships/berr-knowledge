# Qatari-backed fund offers $1.5bn for pizza giant Papa John’s: Report

A Qatari-backed investment fund, Irth Capital, has made a $1.5 billion bid for Papa John’s International at $47 per share, according to Reuters. The offer could mark a significant shift in ownership for the pizza chain.
