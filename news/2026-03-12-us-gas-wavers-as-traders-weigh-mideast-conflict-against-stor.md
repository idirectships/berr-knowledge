# US Gas Wavers as Traders Weigh Mideast Conflict Against Storage

US natural gas prices fluctuated as traders balanced concerns over potential Middle East supply disruptions with expectations of a smaller-than-expected drawdown in storage. The uncertainty highlights the market's sensitivity to geopolitical tensions and inventory data.
