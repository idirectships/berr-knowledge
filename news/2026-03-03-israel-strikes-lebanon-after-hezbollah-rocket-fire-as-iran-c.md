# Israel strikes Lebanon after Hezbollah rocket fire as Iran conflict widens

Israel has struck Lebanon in response to Hezbollah rocket fire, resulting in at least 52 deaths in Lebanon. The escalation raises concerns over the widening conflict involving Iran.
