# Saudi Oil Tanker Giant Snaps Up Ships for Hormuz Workaround

Saudi Arabia’s largest oil shipper is securing tankers at premium rates to route crude via the Red Sea, bypassing the Strait of Hormuz amid ongoing disruptions. The move highlights the strategic shift in global oil logistics due to regional tensions.
