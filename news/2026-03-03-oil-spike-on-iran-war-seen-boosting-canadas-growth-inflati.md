# Oil Spike on Iran War Seen Boosting Canada’s Growth, Inflation

A potential escalation in the Iran war could drive up oil prices, potentially boosting Canada’s economic growth and inflation, according to the Bank of Nova Scotia. The scenario highlights the interconnectedness of global conflicts and energy markets.
