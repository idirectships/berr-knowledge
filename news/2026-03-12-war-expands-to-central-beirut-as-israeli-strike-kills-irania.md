# War expands to central Beirut as Israeli strike kills Iranians in luxury hotel

Israeli strikes in central Beirut killed Iranian nationals, with Israel claiming they were Quds Force operatives and Iran asserting they were diplomats, marking an escalation in the conflict with Hezbollah. The incident raises concerns over regional tensions and potential wider involvement of Iran.
