# Thyssenkrupp Officials Doubt Whether Jindal Steel Deal Can Be Agreed

Thyssenkrupp officials are losing confidence in finalizing a potential sale of its steel division to Jindal Steel International, signaling uncertainty over the deal's future. The move could impact Thyssenkrupp's strategic plans and the broader steel industry.
