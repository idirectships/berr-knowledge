# China approves 'ethnic unity' law requiring minorities to learn Mandarin

China has approved a new law requiring ethnic minorities to learn Mandarin from before kindergarten through high school, signaling a shift in language policy and cultural integration efforts. The law could impact minority communities and their linguistic heritage.
