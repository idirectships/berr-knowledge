# European Gas Prices Follow Oil Higher as Shipping Crisis Worsens

European natural gas prices rose as oil prices increased amid escalating shipping disruptions in the Middle East, raising concerns over prolonged supply chain issues. The situation highlights vulnerabilities in global energy markets during geopolitical tensions.
