# Kidnapping of foreigners soars in Africa's lawless Sahel region

Kidnappings of foreigners in Africa's Sahel region reached record levels in 2025 due to escalating insecurity. The surge highlights the deteriorating security situation and its impact on foreign nationals and regional stability.
