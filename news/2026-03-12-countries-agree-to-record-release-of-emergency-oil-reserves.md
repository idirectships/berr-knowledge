# Countries agree to record release of emergency oil reserves as prices surge

G7 nations have agreed to a record release of emergency oil reserves in response to rising global oil prices triggered by the US-Israel conflict with Iran. The move aims to stabilize markets and mitigate economic impacts from the energy crisis.
