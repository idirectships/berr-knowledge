# 'Even under missiles we carry on living' - how young Iranians are coping with war

Young Iranians are enduring the ongoing US-Israeli bombing campaign by sheltering at home and avoiding public spaces, highlighting the human impact of the conflict. The situation reflects the growing civilian toll and psychological strain amid escalating violence.
