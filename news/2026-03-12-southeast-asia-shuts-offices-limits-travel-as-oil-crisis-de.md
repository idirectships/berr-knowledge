# Southeast Asia shuts offices, limits travel as oil crisis deepens

Southeast Asian nations including Indonesia and Vietnam are closing offices and restricting travel amid worsening fuel shortages linked to a deepening oil crisis. The region's reliance on imported fuel and limited reserves has exacerbated the situation.
