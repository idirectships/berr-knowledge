# Trump says Iran war projected to last 4 to 5 weeks, could go ‘far longer’

Former U.S. President Donald Trump claims a potential war with Iran would last 4 to 5 weeks but could extend much longer, suggesting the U.S. was "very nearly under threat" from Iran. The remarks raise questions about the immediacy of potential conflict.
