# 'Fingers on the trigger': Deadly warnings for Iranians being urged to take action

Iran's police chief has issued a stark warning that security forces will treat protesters as enemies if they are acting on foreign instructions, escalating tensions amid ongoing unrest. The statement underscores the regime's heightened sensitivity to external influence during domestic protests.
