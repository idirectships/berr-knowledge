# French aid worker among three killed in drone strikes in rebel-held DR Congo city

A French aid worker and two others were killed in drone strikes in Goma, a rebel-held city in the Democratic Republic of Congo, according to witnesses. The attack targeted a residential area often inhabited by expatriates and aid workers.
