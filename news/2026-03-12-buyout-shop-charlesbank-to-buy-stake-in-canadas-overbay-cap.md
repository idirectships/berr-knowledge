# Buyout Shop Charlesbank to Buy Stake in Canada’s Overbay Capital

Charlesbank Capital Partners is acquiring a stake in Overbay Capital Partners, valuing the Canadian secondaries firm at C$200 million. The deal signals growing interest in the Canadian private equity market.
