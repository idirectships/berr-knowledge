# Dubai Drone Incidents Add to Regional Flight Disruptions

Dubai International Airport halted operations after drone incidents caused injuries, adding to flight disruptions linked to the ongoing conflict with Iran. The region has seen over 46,000 flight cancellations since the conflict began.
