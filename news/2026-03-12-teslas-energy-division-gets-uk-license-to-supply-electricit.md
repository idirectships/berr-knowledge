# Tesla’s Energy Division Gets UK License to Supply Electricity

Tesla’s energy division has received a UK license to supply electricity, expanding its reach into the British market for residential and commercial customers. This marks a significant step in Tesla’s efforts to grow its energy services outside the U.S.
