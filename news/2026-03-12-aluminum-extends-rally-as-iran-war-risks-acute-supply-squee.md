# Aluminum Extends Rally as Iran War Risks ‘Acute Supply Squeeze’

Aluminum prices rose as tensions in the Middle East heightened fears of supply disruptions from regional producers. The escalation raises concerns over potential shortages and further price increases in a key industrial metal.
