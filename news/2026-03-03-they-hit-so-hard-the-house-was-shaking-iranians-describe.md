# 'They hit so hard the house was shaking': Iranians describe impact of US-Israel attacks

Iranians report intense explosions and shaking buildings as US and Israeli strikes continue into a third day. The attacks have caused significant civilian disruption and raised concerns over escalating regional conflict.
