# Hong Kong Arrests Hedge Fund, Brokerage Staff in Insider Probe

Hong Kong authorities arrested staff from a hedge fund and two brokerage firms in an insider trading probe, signaling a significant crackdown on financial misconduct. The investigation could impact market integrity and regulatory enforcement in the region.
