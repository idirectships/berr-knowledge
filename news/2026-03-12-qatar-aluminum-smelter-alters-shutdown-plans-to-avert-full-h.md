# Qatar Aluminum Smelter Alters Shutdown Plans to Avert Full Halt

Qatar Aluminum Ltd. has revised its shutdown plans for its smelter, maintaining operations at 60% capacity after securing continued gas supply, avoiding a full halt. The decision impacts regional aluminum production and energy reliance.
