# Brent crude oil briefly tops $100 a barrel as Iran attacks on shipping worsen supply concerns

Brent crude oil briefly exceeded $100 a barrel as Iranian attacks on shipping in the Strait of Hormuz intensified supply concerns, pushing global energy markets higher. The U.S. airstrikes in Iran have entered their 13th day, heightening regional tensions.
