# Deutsche Bank Sees Higher Litigation Charges Amid Paschi Lawsuit

Deutsche Bank anticipates increased litigation costs due to a lawsuit with former managers over its role in the Banca Monte dei Paschi di Siena accounting scandal. The case could impact the bank's financial results and reputation.
