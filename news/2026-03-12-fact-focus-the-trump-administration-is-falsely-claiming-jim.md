# FACT FOCUS: The Trump administration is falsely claiming Jimmy Carter was against mail-in voting

The Trump administration is misrepresenting former President Jimmy Carter’s stance on mail-in voting, using a 20-year-old report to support legislation requiring strict voter ID laws. The claim has implications for ongoing debates over election integrity and voting access.
