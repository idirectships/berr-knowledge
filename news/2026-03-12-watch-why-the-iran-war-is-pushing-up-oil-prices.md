# Watch: Why the Iran war is pushing up oil prices

Oil prices surged above $100 a barrel as tensions in the Middle East, particularly involving Iran, heightened fears of potential disruptions to global energy supplies. The increase reflects market concerns over regional stability and its impact on oil exports.
