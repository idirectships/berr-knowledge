# Noma head chef resigns from restaurant amid abuse allegations

René Redzepi, head chef of Noma, has resigned from the restaurant amid allegations of creating a toxic work environment. The resignation follows previous apologies and highlights ongoing concerns about workplace culture in high-profile culinary institutions.
