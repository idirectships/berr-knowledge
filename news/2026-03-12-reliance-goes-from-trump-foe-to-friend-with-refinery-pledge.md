# Reliance Goes From Trump Foe to Friend With Refinery Pledge

Reliance Industries Ltd. has shifted its stance from opposing Donald Trump to aligning with him, following a pledge to invest in an oil refinery. The move signals a potential realignment of business and political ties ahead of the 2024 U.S. election.
