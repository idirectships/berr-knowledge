# What Next for Oil Prices as Brent Jumps Back Above $100

Brent crude oil prices have risen above $100 per barrel amid heightened tensions from the Iran war spreading across the Middle East, raising concerns over potential disruptions to global oil supplies. The situation could influence energy markets and inflationary pressures.
