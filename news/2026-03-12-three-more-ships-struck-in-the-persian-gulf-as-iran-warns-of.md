# Three more ships struck in the Persian Gulf as Iran warns of oil prices hitting $200

Three additional ships were struck in the Persian Gulf, raising tensions in the Strait of Hormuz amid Iran's warning of potential oil prices reaching $200. The incidents could disrupt global oil flows and escalate regional conflict.
