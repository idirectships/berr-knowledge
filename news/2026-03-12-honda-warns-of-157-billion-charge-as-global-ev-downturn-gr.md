# Honda Warns of $15.7 Billion Charge as Global EV Downturn Grows

Honda Motor Co. announced a potential $15.7 billion charge as it reevaluates its electric vehicle strategy amid a global slowdown in the EV transition. The move highlights growing financial pressures on automakers adapting to shifting market conditions.
