# Indian Bourse NSE Picks Bankers, Lawyers for $2.5 Billion IPO

The National Stock Exchange of India has selected 20 bankers for its $2.5 billion IPO, a major step in its long-awaited transition to a publicly traded entity. The move signals a significant shift in India's financial markets.
