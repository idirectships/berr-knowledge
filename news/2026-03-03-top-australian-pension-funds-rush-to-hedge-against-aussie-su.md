# Top Australian Pension Funds Rush to Hedge Against Aussie Surge

Australia's largest pension funds are actively hedging against a rising Australian dollar, which risks reducing returns on their $4.5 trillion retirement portfolios. The move highlights growing concerns over currency volatility impacting long-term investments.
