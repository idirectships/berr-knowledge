# Live updates: Iran commander says Strait of Hormuz closed, vows to hit ships trying to pass, report says

An Iranian commander claims the Strait of Hormuz is closed and vows to attack ships attempting to pass, according to a report, escalating tensions following a U.S.-Israel strike that killed Iran's Supreme Leader. The move could disrupt global oil flows and heighten regional conflict.
