# Nvidia to invest $4 billion into photonics companies Coherent and Lumentum

Nvidia has committed $4 billion in investments to photonics firms Coherent and Lumentum, with each receiving $2 billion, signaling a major strategic move in the chipmaker's expansion into advanced optical technologies.
