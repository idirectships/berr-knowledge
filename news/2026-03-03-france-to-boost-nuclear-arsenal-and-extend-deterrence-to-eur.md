# France to boost nuclear arsenal and extend deterrence to European allies

France plans to expand its nuclear arsenal and extend nuclear deterrence to eight European allies, according to President Emmanuel Macron, while maintaining sole control over nuclear decisions. The move signals a shift in European security strategy amid rising geopolitical tensions.
