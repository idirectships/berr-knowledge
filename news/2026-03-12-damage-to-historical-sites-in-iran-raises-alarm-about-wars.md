# Damage to historical sites in Iran raises alarm about war’s impact on protected places

U.S. and Israeli strikes have damaged at least four cultural and historical sites in Iran, including palaces and an ancient mosque, raising concerns about the war's impact on protected heritage. The damage highlights the broader consequences of the escalating conflict.
