# Geopolitics Bets Hit a Record on Polymarket as Iran War Escalates

Geopolitical betting on Polymarket reached a record as tensions between the US-Israeli alliance and Iran escalated, reflecting heightened uncertainty over potential conflict outcomes. Analysts noted unusual trading patterns, raising concerns about market manipulation.
