# UAE Aluminum Maker Looks to Overseas Stocks to Avoid Delays

Emirates Global Aluminum is exploring the use of overseas stocks to mitigate shipping delays caused by the war in Iran, ensuring timely deliveries to customers. The move highlights the impact of geopolitical tensions on global supply chains.
