# Airbnb Plans High-Grade Bond Debut as Convertible Deal Comes Due

Airbnb is preparing for its first high-grade bond offering as its existing convertible notes approach maturity, signaling a shift in its capital structure strategy. The move could impact its debt profile and investor confidence.
