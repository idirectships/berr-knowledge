# Voya Limits Data Center Credit Investing on AI Demand Worries

Voya Financial is scaling back investments in data center credits due to concerns that AI-driven demand for data centers may peak before existing debts are repaid. The move reflects growing caution in the sector amid potential slowdowns in AI infrastructure growth.
