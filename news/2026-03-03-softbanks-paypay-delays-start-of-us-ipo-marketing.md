# SoftBank’s PayPay Delays Start of US IPO Marketing

PayPay, a SoftBank-backed digital payments company, has delayed the start of its US IPO marketing, signaling a potential shift in its timeline for going public. The move may reflect ongoing challenges in the competitive fintech landscape.
