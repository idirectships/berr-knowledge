# Steven Major Sees Bonds Facing Stagflation Scenario

Steven Major, a global macro advisor, warns that the bond market is increasingly pricing in stagflation risks, signaling concerns over simultaneous inflation and economic stagnation. This highlights growing uncertainty in global financial markets.
