# ‘Life covered in soot’: Gas shortage forces Gaza families to cook over wood

A severe gas shortage in Gaza has forced families to burn wood and plastic for cooking, exposing them to harmful smoke and health risks. The crisis highlights the deteriorating living conditions in the region.
