# Bangladesh parliament meets after uprising, elections ushered in new gov’t

Bangladesh's parliament has reconvened for the first time since an uprising, with newly elected Prime Minister Rahman accusing former leader Sheikh Hasina of undermining the previous government. The shift marks a significant political transition following recent elections.
