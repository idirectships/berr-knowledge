# UN Security Council adopts resolution condemning Iran’s attacks in the Gulf

The UN Security Council has adopted a resolution condemning Iran's attacks in the Gulf, backed by 135 co-sponsoring countries. The move signals broad international concern over regional tensions and potential escalation.
