# Iran targets Gulf nations with missiles, drones as oil prices soar

Iran launched missile and drone attacks on Gulf nations, with Bahrain reporting strikes on fuel tanks and Saudi Arabia intercepting drones targeting an oilfield, causing oil prices to rise sharply. The incident escalates regional tensions and raises concerns over energy security.
