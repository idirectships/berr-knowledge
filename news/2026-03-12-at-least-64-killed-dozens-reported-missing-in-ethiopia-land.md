# At least 64 killed, dozens reported missing in Ethiopia landslides, floods

At least 64 people have been killed and dozens are missing in Ethiopia due to landslides and floods, with authorities reporting many victims found buried in mud. The disaster highlights the immediate human toll and potential for further casualties in the region.
