# Oil Diverges From Emerging-Market Currencies by Most on Record

Oil prices have diverged from emerging-market currencies to the greatest extent on record, signaling a major shift in global market dynamics driven by the Iran conflict. The negative correlation reflects heightened geopolitical risk and economic uncertainty.
