# Netanyahu: Iran would ‘threaten all of humanity’ with nuclear weapon

Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu warned that Iran would "threaten all of humanity" with a nuclear weapon, following a visit to a site where an Iranian missile strike killed nine people. The statement underscores escalating tensions between Israel and Iran.
