# Foreigners Sell Most Net Japan Stocks, Futures Since November

Foreign investors sold the most net Japanese stocks and futures since November, driven by a risk-off sentiment following the outbreak of the Iran war. The move reflects heightened global market volatility and geopolitical concerns.
