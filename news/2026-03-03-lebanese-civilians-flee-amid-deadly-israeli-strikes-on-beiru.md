# Lebanese civilians flee amid deadly Israeli strikes on Beirut suburbs

Israeli airstrikes in Beirut suburbs have killed at least 31 people, prompting mass evacuations and gridlocked highways as Lebanese civilians flee. The assault marks Israel's deadliest attack on Lebanon in over a year.
