# Deutsche Bank Says Commercial Real Estate Remains Key Risk

Deutsche Bank warns that its exposure to US commercial real estate remains a key risk, potentially leading to higher credit loss provisions due to ongoing refinancing challenges and uncertain price stabilization. The assessment highlights continued vulnerabilities in the sector.
