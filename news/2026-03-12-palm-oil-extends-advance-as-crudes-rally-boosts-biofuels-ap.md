# Palm Oil Extends Advance as Crude’s Rally Boosts Biofuels Appeal

Palm oil futures rose as crude oil prices climbed above $100, boosting demand for biofuel feedstocks. The move reflects increased interest in alternative energy sources amid higher fossil fuel prices.
