# Bond Traders Are Pricing in Bigger Deficits Around the World

Global bond yields are rising as traders anticipate larger fiscal deficits, signaling increased borrowing costs and potential economic shifts. The 30-year US Treasury bond yield approaches 4.9%, reflecting broader market expectations.
