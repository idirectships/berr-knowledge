# Qatar Downs Pair of Iranian Fighter Jets in First for Conflict

Qatar’s air force shot down two Iranian Su-24 fighter jets, marking the first confirmed destruction of Iranian crewed aircraft in the ongoing conflict. The incident escalates tensions between the two nations.
