# France walks ‘fine line’ as US-Israel war on Iran escalates

French President Emmanuel Macron has criticized the escalating US-Israel war on Iran but stopped short of condemning the conflict outright, instead blaming Iran for the situation. The remarks highlight France's delicate balancing act between its alliance with the US and its diplomatic ties with Iran.
