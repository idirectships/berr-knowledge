# Daimler Truck Says Higher Orders, Savings to Offset Tariffs

Daimler Truck plans deeper cost cuts and expects higher sales to offset increased tariff expenses, aiming to maintain earnings at last year's levels. The move highlights the impact of trade tensions on global automotive manufacturing.
