# JPMorgan's Dimon Not Surprised By Market Reaction to Iran

JPMorgan CEO Jamie Dimon expressed no surprise at the market's calm response to recent strikes in Iran, indicating potential underestimation of geopolitical risks. The comment highlights ongoing concerns about market complacency toward regional tensions.
