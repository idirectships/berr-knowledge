# Could Kim's teen daughter become North Korea's next leader?

North Korea's Kim Jong Un has increasingly shown his daughter, Kim Jue Ae, in public appearances, raising speculation about her potential future role in the country's leadership. The move signals a possible shift in the regime's succession planning.
