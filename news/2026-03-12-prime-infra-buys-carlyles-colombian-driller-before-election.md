# Prime Infra Buys Carlyle’s Colombian Driller Before Election

Prime Infrastructure Capital Inc. of the Philippines has acquired Colombia’s largest independent oil producer from Carlyle Group ahead of the country’s presidential election, which may influence future energy policies. The deal signals strategic investment in Colombia’s energy sector amid political uncertainty.
