# Indonesia, Australia to expand security cooperation with Japan, PNG

Indonesia and Australia are expanding security cooperation with Japan and Papua New Guinea, with plans to establish a joint training facility on Morotai Island. The move signals growing regional defense partnerships in the Indo-Pacific.
