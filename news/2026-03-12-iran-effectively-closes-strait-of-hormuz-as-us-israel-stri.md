# Iran effectively closes Strait of Hormuz as U.S.-Israel strikes continue

Iran has effectively closed the Strait of Hormuz amid ongoing U.S.-Israel airstrikes, causing what officials call the largest oil disruption in history. The move could significantly impact global oil markets and regional stability.
