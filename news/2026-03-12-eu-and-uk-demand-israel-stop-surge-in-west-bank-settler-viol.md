# EU and UK demand Israel stop surge in West Bank settler violence since Iran war

The EU and UK have called on Israel to halt an increase in settler violence in the West Bank amid the ongoing conflict with Iran. Six Palestinians have been killed in attacks by settlers since the war began, according to the UN.
