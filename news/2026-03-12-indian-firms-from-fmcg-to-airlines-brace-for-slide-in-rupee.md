# Indian Firms From FMCG to Airlines Brace for Slide in Rupee

Indian companies across sectors including FMCG and airlines are preparing for a potential slide in the rupee, with Elara Securities forecasting the currency could weaken to 94–95 per dollar. A weaker rupee increases import costs and inflationary pressures, impacting corporate margins
