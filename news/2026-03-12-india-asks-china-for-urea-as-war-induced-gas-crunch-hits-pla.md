# India Asks China For Urea as War-Induced Gas Crunch Hits Plants

India is seeking Chinese urea exports as a war-induced gas shortage disrupts domestic fertilizer production, impacting agricultural output. The move highlights growing energy and supply chain vulnerabilities amid regional conflicts.
