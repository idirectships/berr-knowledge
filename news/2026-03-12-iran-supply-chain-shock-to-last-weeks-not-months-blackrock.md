# Iran Supply Chain Shock to Last Weeks, Not Months, BlackRock’s Boivin Says

BlackRock’s Jean Boivin estimates the supply chain disruption from the Iran conflict will last weeks, not months, suggesting a more limited impact than previously feared. The assessment affects expectations for global energy markets and inflation.
