# Some Middle East flights resume, but thousands of travelers are still stranded by war

Limited Middle East flights have resumed following U.S. and Israeli attacks on Iran, but hundreds of thousands of travelers remain stranded due to ongoing safety concerns. The conflict is disrupting global travel and raising fears of further escalation.
