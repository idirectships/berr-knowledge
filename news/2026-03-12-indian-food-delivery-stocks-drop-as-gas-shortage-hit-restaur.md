# Indian Food Delivery Stocks Drop as Gas Shortage Hit Restaurants

Indian food delivery stocks fell as restaurants face operational disruptions due to a cooking-gas shortage linked to the Middle East conflict. The crisis highlights the ripple effects of geopolitical tensions on supply chains and consumer services.
